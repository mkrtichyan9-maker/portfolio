Starting Here: Why I Built This Portfolio and What It's For

Every portfolio is an act of translation — turning work, thinking, and decisions into something legible to someone who wasn't there.

Every portfolio is an act of translation. You take work you've done — thinking, research, decisions, failures, iterations — and you find the words and structures that make it legible to someone who wasn't there.

This portfolio is mine. It documents my journey from a BA in Advertising and PR at the Russian-Armenian University to an MA in Content Strategy at FH Joanneum in Austria. A journey shaped by a single question: how does content shape the way people perceive things?

How does a visual choice change a brand? How does a word, a tone, a structure change how an audience feels? These are the questions I carry into every project I work on.

I built this site for people working in content strategy, branding, and digital communication — future employers, collaborators, and anyone curious about what it means to approach content strategically rather than reactively.

What you'll find here is not a highlight reel. It's a structured record of real work — projects presented honestly, with a focus on the challenge, the approach, and what I learned. I believe that reflection is not a soft skill; it is the mechanism by which experience becomes knowledge.

This is the first post. There will be more. Welcome — and thank you for reading.
