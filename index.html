About Me

Marketing & PR specialist focused on brand perception.

I am a marketing and PR specialist focused on branding and content strategy. My main interest lies in how visual content shapes the way people perceive brands — especially on social media platforms like Instagram.

Through academic research and practical work, I explore how different visual styles influence brand identity and audience perception. In my recent thesis project, I analysed the impact of visual content on brand perception and created several visual concepts to test how design choices change brand interpretation.

My goal is to develop strategies that combine creativity with clear communication — helping brands build a recognisable and meaningful presence online.
